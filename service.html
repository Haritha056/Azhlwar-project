GST return is a document that will contain all the details of your sales, purchases, tax collected on sales (output tax), and tax paid on purchases (input tax).Once you file GST returns, you will need to pay the resulting tax liability (money that you owe the government).

Income Tax Filing
ITR-1, ITR-2, ITR-3...

Income Tax Return (ITR) is a form which a person is supposed to submit to the Income Tax Department of India.As per the income tax laws, the return must be filed every year by an individual or business that earns any income during a financial year.
